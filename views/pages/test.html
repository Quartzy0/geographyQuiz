General info about the UAE


The founding

The UAE is formed by seven emirates and was founded on the 2 December 1971 as a federation. Six of the seven emirates (Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain and Fujairah) combined on that date. The seventh, Ras al Khaimah, joined the federation on 10 February 1972.


Climate

The rainfall amount is generally around 100 mm a year


Languages

The official language spoken in the UAE is Arabic and the most popular foreign language spoken is English.


Typical food

Some of the typixal food is: Stuffed Camel, Al Harees, Shawarma and Al Machboos


Biomes

The main biome in all of United Arab Emirates is the hot desert biome


Culture

The UAE is a predominantly Muslim country.
